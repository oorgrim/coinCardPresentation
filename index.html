History of Bitcoin

Bitcoin was created in 2009 by an unknown person using the name Satoshi Nakamoto. The first transaction was recorded shortly after its release.

2009: Bitcoin is Born

Bitcoin was created by the pseudonymous Satoshi Nakamoto and the first transaction on the Bitcoin blockchain occurred.

2011: Bitcoin's First Major Adoption

Bitcoin starts being accepted by more businesses and the price reaches $1 for the first time.

2013: The First Bitcoin Exchange

The first Bitcoin exchange is launched, allowing for Bitcoin trading on a larger scale.

2017: Bitcoin Reaches $20,000

Bitcoin hits an all-time high of $20,000, bringing global attention to the cryptocurrency market.

2021: Bitcoin Hits New Heights

Bitcoin reaches new highs, with many companies adding Bitcoin to their balance sheets as a store of value.
